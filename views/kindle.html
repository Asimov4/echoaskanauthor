Payments
    Payment Number (What's this?)
Unique identifier for this payment transaction. Use this number when contacting Amazon customer service with questions about your royalty payments.
Sales Period Marketplace Payment Status (What's this?)
View the payment status of your transactions. This column will display ‘Pending’ while a payment is being processed by your bank. Once processing is complete, the status will change to ‘Paid’. In the unlikely event that the payment was rejected by your bank, this column will display the status as ‘Failed’. Learn more
Date Payment Method Accrued Royalty Tax Withholding (What's this?)
This column contains tax withholding amounts wherever applicable. For more information on tax withholding rates and Amazon policies, please visit the ‘Tax Information’ section of your KDP Account page.
Adjustments (What's this?)
This column will indicate any payment adjustments that may occur to offset overpayment or underpayment of past royalties. This may be due to refunds or technical issues at processing banks and occur infrequently.
Net Earnings (What's this?)
This column shows your net earnings after tax deductions and any adjustments in the marketplace currency where the sale was recorded.
FX Rate (What's this?)
This column provides the conversion rate applied to your net earnings to arrive at your final payment amount, if it is different from the marketplace currency in which the sale was recorded. The column will display ‘Pending’ while your payments are being processed and will automatically update once exchange rate information is available. For cases where rates are not applicable, the column will display ‘N/A’.
Payment (What's this?)
This column provides the final amount that you will be paid. The column will display ‘Pending’ while your transactions are being processed and will automatically update once your bank confirms receipt of your payment.
